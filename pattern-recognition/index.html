Pattern Recognition

Pattern Recognition explores the universal rhythms that underlie intelligence, consciousness, and learning - patterns that emerge not just in artificial neural networks or human brains, but across all scales of existence. Through generative algorithms that embody these patterns, each piece reveals how concepts like attention, memory, and emergence transcend our artificial categorizations of natural versus artificial, digital versus biological.
